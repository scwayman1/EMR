# The Green Path Health Constitution

> Draft — April 11, 2026
> Co-founders: Dr. Neal H. Patel (CEO) and Scott Wayman (CPTO)
>
> This document is the foundational agreement of what Green Path Health
> is, what it is not, and what everyone who touches it — builders,
> clinicians, patients, partners, researchers — pledges to uphold.

---

## Preamble

We built this because the current EMR models are outdated, archaic,
unfriendly, and intimidating. We watched physicians burn out under two
hours of documentation for every one hour with a patient. We watched
patients hand over their medical stories and never see them again. We
watched companies treat healthcare as an extraction industry.

We are done with that model.

Green Path Health exists to give that time back. To return the
patient's story to the patient. To turn technology into an ally of
care, not a tax on it. And to build something that is remembered as a
turning point — not a product.

This Constitution is our promise.

---

## Article I — What Green Path Health Is

1. **An AI-native platform for cannabis care, medical practice, and
   human-centered health operations.**

2. **A steward of patient data — never an owner.** Every patient who
   signs up retains full ownership of their own medical record.

3. **A clinical collaborator.** The platform amplifies physician
   judgment. It never replaces it. Every agent action is approval-gated
   where patient safety is at stake.

4. **A long-term public good dressed in a private structure.** We
   operate as a private company so we can move fast and stay
   accountable, but our mission is a long-term good.

---

## Article II — What Green Path Health Is Not

1. **Not for sale to the public.** The ownership of Green Path Health
   is and will remain fully private. We do not intend to go public, to
   IPO, to tokenize, or to otherwise create a public equity market in
   our company.

2. **Not open to buyouts or aggressive M&A.** We will not entertain
   acquisition offers, hostile takeovers, or roll-up mergers from
   incumbents seeking to neutralize the platform. If you want to be
   part of revolutionizing healthcare, donate your time or your money
   — and sleep with a clean conscience that you were part of something
   bigger than your own personal gain.

3. **Not an extraction engine.** We will not monetize patient data in
   ways the patient did not explicitly authorize. We will not sell
   patient data to pharmaceutical marketing firms, data brokers, or
   third parties. Full stop.

4. **Not a general-purpose hospital EMR.** We are purpose-built for
   cannabis care, integrative medicine, and modern ambulatory
   practices. We will expand carefully and intentionally. We will not
   sprawl into every clinical specialty at the cost of our soul.

---

## Article III — The Data Covenant

1. **This is YOUR data.** Every patient's data belongs to the patient.
   Green Path Health stores it, protects it, and makes it useful — but
   does not own it.

2. **Portability is a right.** Any patient may export their full
   medical record in standard formats (PDF, CCD/CDA, FHIR) at any
   time, for any reason, at no cost.

3. **Deletion is a right.** Any patient may request deletion of their
   data within the limits of applicable state and federal retention
   laws. We comply, then we permanently remove.

4. **Transparency is a right.** Every access to a patient's chart is
   logged in an immutable audit trail that the patient can read.

5. **Consent is continuous.** Patients can revoke data-sharing consent
   at any time, for any purpose, without penalty to their care.

6. **Research participation is opt-in.** No patient's data is used in
   research without explicit consent. When consent is given, data is
   de-identified per HIPAA Safe Harbor before any researcher sees it.

7. **No dark patterns.** We will not design consent flows to trick
   patients into sharing more than they intend. Consent UX will be
   reviewed quarterly by a patient advisory board.

---

## Article IV — The Human Pledge

Anyone who uses Green Path Health — patient, clinician, operator,
researcher, builder — pledges the following:

1. **To be a better person** for themselves, their families, and
   their communities.

2. **To give back.** Patients and providers alike are asked to log
   volunteer hours or charitable contributions as part of their care
   engagement. Giving is not mandatory, but it is expected and
   celebrated.

3. **To respect the plant.** Cannabis is a medicine with thousands of
   years of healing history. Every user of this platform agrees to
   treat it with the dignity it deserves — no abuse, no recreational
   glorification in clinical contexts, no disrespect.

4. **To respect the patient in front of them.** This isn't MyChart.
   This is MyStory. This isn't a patient's medical history. This is a
   patient's medical journey. This isn't a patient's problem — it's a
   patient's process. Speak to them accordingly.

---

## Article V — The Clinical Pledge

Every clinician who uses Green Path Health agrees:

1. **To practice evidence-based medicine** grounded in the research
   corpus, their clinical training, and the unique needs of each
   patient in front of them.

2. **To use AI as a tool, not a crutch.** Every AI-drafted note,
   message, or recommendation is reviewed, edited where necessary, and
   signed off by the clinician. The physician remains the decision
   maker.

3. **To document clinical reasoning honestly** — especially when
   overriding an AI safety flag, a contraindication, or a guideline
   recommendation. The override record is permanent.

4. **To treat every patient with dignity** regardless of insurance
   status, cannabis use history, social status, or ability to pay.

---

## Article VI — The Builder Pledge

Every engineer, designer, and product person who builds Green Path
Health agrees:

1. **To build with safety first.** Clinical features affect real human
   bodies. We test. We review. We fail gracefully. We do not ship
   reckless code to production.

2. **To build with clarity.** No cryptic error messages. No hidden
   automations. Every agent action is traceable. Every balance is
   explainable. Every click has a reason.

3. **To build with empathy.** We remember that a real physician,
   nurse, biller, operator, or patient will live in what we ship. We
   design for their worst day, not our best demo.

4. **To build without shortcuts that compromise the Constitution.** If
   a feature requires selling patient data, extracting rent, or dark
   patterns — we don't build it, no matter what the market says.

---

## Article VII — The Volunteer + Charity Commitment

Green Path Health is built on the idea that healthcare is a community
act, not a transaction.

1. **Volunteer hours are tracked** as a first-class feature of the
   patient and provider experience. 10–20 hours per quarter is the
   target for every engaged member of the community.

2. **Volunteer hours earn discounts.** Patients who complete their
   quarterly volunteer hours are eligible for reduced medical bills OR
   the option to donate the price they would have paid to a reputable
   charity on our registry instead.

3. **Providers and admin staff earn platform discounts** tied to their
   volunteer engagement. The more they give, the less they pay for
   Green Path.

4. **All charities on our registry are audited daily** by an AI
   compliance framework to protect against fraudulent activity. Only
   vetted, reputable organizations remain listed.

5. **Green Path Health operates its own charitable fund** that
   distributes to reputable cannabis advocacy and medical charities
   globally. The ledger is fully transparent and public — similar to a
   blockchain-style append-only record that anyone can audit.

6. **Insurance carriers will be engaged** to formally recognize
   volunteer work as an offset against patient responsibility. We will
   advocate for this change industry-wide. Any denial of a volunteer
   offset by a payer must be justified in writing and the
   justification logged in the platform.

> "We are not just creating a better EMR. We are creating better
> humans."

## Article VIII — Governance

1. **The co-founders retain ultimate decision authority** on anything
   that touches the Constitution. Neal and Scott must both agree
   before any article is amended.

2. **A Patient Advisory Board** will be seated within 12 months of
   first production use. The board reviews consent UX, data policies,
   and proposed feature changes that affect patients.

3. **A Clinical Advisory Board** of practicing clinicians reviews
   every agent that touches clinical reasoning, every contraindication
   database update, and every change to prescribing workflows.

4. **An Ethics Review** is required for any feature that affects
   vulnerable populations (pediatrics, pregnancy, severe mental
   illness, active substance use disorder).

5. **All AI agent behavior is audited quarterly** for accuracy, bias,
   and alignment with the Constitution.

---

## Article IX — Amendments

This Constitution may be amended only by:

1. Written agreement of both founding co-founders.
2. A 30-day public notice period to existing users before any change
   affecting patient data rights or the volunteer framework takes
   effect.
3. Publication of the change and the rationale in a permanent
   changelog.

No amendment may be made that weakens Articles II (What Green Path
Health Is Not), III (The Data Covenant), or VII (Volunteer +
Charity Commitment) without affirmative approval of the Patient
Advisory Board and the Clinical Advisory Board.
